7 Columbia home bakeries

During the pandemic, home bakeries became a popular way for people to explore a new hobby and be productive while stuck at home. They have remained in the public eye ever since, and with the 2022 changes to Missouri’s cottage food laws, it is now easier to convert a home kitchen into a successful business. Here’s a snapshot of Columbia’s home baking community.
